# ⚖️ LegalEase

A modern legal service platform that connects clients, legal seekers, and businesses with verified lawyers. Clients can browse lawyer profiles, hire legal professionals, and manage their hiring history, while lawyers can showcase and manage their services after completing a one-time verification payment. An admin panel is available to oversee and manage the entire platform.

## 🌐 Live Website

**Client:** https://lawyerplatformclient.vercel.app/

---

## 🎯 Purpose

Traditional legal service hiring is often time-consuming and lacks transparency. LegalEase simplifies this process by providing a secure online platform where users can discover, compare, and hire verified lawyers based on their expertise. The platform also enables lawyers to manage their services efficiently while giving administrators full control over the system.

---

## ✨ Key Features

- 🔐 Secure authentication with Email/Password and Google Login.
- 👥 Role-based registration (Client & Lawyer).
- ⚖️ Browse and search verified lawyers.
- 💼 Hire lawyers through an easy hiring system.
- 📜 Clients can view their hiring history.
- 🧑‍⚖️ Lawyers can manage their legal services.
- 💳 One-time lawyer verification payment before offering services.
- 🛡️ Protected routes using JWT authentication.
- 📱 Fully responsive design for mobile, tablet, and desktop.
- 🎨 Clean and recruiter-friendly UI with proper spacing and alignment.
